Signal Rules

Signal 1: Away team on back-to-back + home team rested 3 or more days → fade the away team. ROI: +8.7% across 79 games.

Signal 2: Away team on back-to-back + confirmed backup goalie starting → fade the away team. ROI: +38.7% across 39 games. Higher variance — use smaller unit sizing.

Cancellation Rule: When both teams are on a back-to-back, all signals are void.

Partial Signal: Away B2B + home rested exactly 2 days = lower confidence. Track but do not act at full unit size.
