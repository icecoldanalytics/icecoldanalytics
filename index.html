Signal Rules & Methodology

Signal 1 — Fatigue Edge: Fires when the away team is on a back-to-back and the home team has been rested for 3 or more days. We fade the away team.


Signal 1 Partial — Moderate Fatigue Edge: Away team on back-to-back, home team rested exactly 2 days. Lower confidence — reduced unit sizing recommended.


Signal 2 — Goalie Edge: Fires when Signal 1 conditions are met AND the away team's backup goalie is confirmed starting. Higher variance — use smaller unit sizing.


Cancellation Rule: When both teams are on a back-to-back, all signals are void.
