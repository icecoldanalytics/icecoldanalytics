Responsible Gambling

If you or someone you know has a gambling problem, help is available right now:

  • Call or text 1-800-522-4700 — free, confidential, 24/7
  • Alberta Health Services Addiction Helpline: 1-866-332-2322
  • AGLC Self-Exclusion — Alberta's regulator offers a self-exclusion program covering licensed gambling in the province; details at aglc.ca
  • Every licensed Alberta sportsbook is required to offer deposit limits, time-outs, and self-exclusion tools in your account settings — use them

Ice Cold Analytics publishes historically tracked betting signals and statistical models. These are patterns observed in past data — they are not predictions, guarantees, or financial advice, and past performance does not guarantee future results. Never bet money you cannot afford to lose. Sports betting should be entertainment; if it stops feeling that way, stop and reach out to the resources above. You must be 18 or older to use gambling products in Alberta.

Who We Are

Ice Cold Analytics (icecoldanalytics.ca) is an independent NHL analytics platform based in Edmonton, Alberta, Canada. We are not a sportsbook, we never take bets, and we do not handle wagers or gambling funds of any kind. We publish analytics, tracked signal results, and statistical models for informational and entertainment purposes.

Privacy Policy

What we collect

  • Email address — only if you voluntarily join our beta waitlist or email list. Collected and stored via Brevo, our email service provider.
  • Basic site analytics — standard, aggregate web traffic information (page views, approximate region) as provided by our hosting platform. We do not build individual profiles of visitors.

What we do with it

  • Emails are used to send the content you signed up for (signal alerts, product updates). Every email includes an unsubscribe link, and unsubscribing is honored immediately.
  • We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information. Ever.
  • If you sign up with a sportsbook through a referral link on our site, that operator's own privacy policy governs whatever you share with them — we receive at most anonymous, aggregate referral reporting.

Your choices

You can unsubscribe from emails at any time via the link in any email. To request deletion of your email from our list entirely, contact us (below) and we'll action it promptly.
